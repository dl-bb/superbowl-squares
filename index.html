Superbowl Squares

How to Win

Winners are determined by matching the last digit of each team's score at the end of each quarter.

For example: If the score is Chiefs 17, 49ers 13, the winning square would be where 7 (Chiefs) and 3 (49ers) intersect.

Prizes awarded at: End of 1st Quarter, Halftime, End of 3rd Quarter, and Final Score
